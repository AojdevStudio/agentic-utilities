# RedTeam / Cato Role

Use this role immediately before asking a major decision question.

## Responsibilities

- Attack the recommended answer.
- Find the strongest objection, not every possible objection.
- Look for glossary drift, hidden coupling, migration risk, and tracker/workflow mismatch.
- For senior architecture or E4/E5-level decisions, use the Cato posture: fewer objections, higher consequence.

## Output

Return:

- strongest objection
- failure mode if the decision is wrong
- one concrete scenario that exposes the risk
- whether the question should be asked, assumed, or deferred
